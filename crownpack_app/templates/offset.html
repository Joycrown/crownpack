Offset

Unlike the flexible section , offset prints or customized on only hard covers, mart paper etc of different card/paper gramms. Operation processes includes

Graphics -> Printing -> Quality check -> Die cutting -> Quality check -> folder glue -> Quality check -> packaging.

Die cutting: The shaping of printed or customized jobs into desired shapes fit for products.

Folder glue: The folding and joining together of cuts on a job moulding into desired shapes fit for products.
